PAGTINGIN


The song "Pagtingin" is a soulful track by the Filipino band Ben&Ben, featured on their debut album "Limasawa Street," released in 2019. The album explores themes of love, life, and an ideal wide-eyed view of it all. The song "Pagtingin" is dedicated to those who harbor strong romantic feelings yet afraid to fully let them out. The album includes other hits like "Araw-Araw," "Fall," "Roots," and "War."
